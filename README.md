# Counting Zoo 🦁🐯🐘

### Supporting, Not Sorting: Building Foundations for Confidence.

## 📖 The Mission
As a licensed educator with a Master’s in Pedagogy, I have taught in Charter, Private, Public, and International school systems. Throughout my career, I noticed a recurring trend: children are often pushed forward into abstract concepts before their **foundational skills** are solid. 

When we sort children strictly by grade level rather than supporting their individual mastery, they don't just miss a skill—they lose their **confidence**. **Counting Zoo** is designed to bring learning "Back to Basics," ensuring every child builds the unshakeable foundation needed for higher-level abstract thinking.

## 🎯 Educational Focus: Florida B.E.S.T. MA.K.NSO.1.1
This app is specifically engineered to address the core requirements of early number sense:
- **1-to-1 Correspondence:** Precise tactile counting of up to 20 objects.
- **Principle of Rearrangement:** Helping students understand that the quantity remains the same even when the layout changes.
- **Subitizing & Grouping:** Automatically organizing objects into clusters of 2, 3, and 5 to move beyond simple counting and into composite thinking.
- **Accessible Numerals:** Reinforcing number formation with clean, "International Standard" straight lines (vertical 1s and hook-free 5s) to reduce fine-motor confusion.

## ✨ Pedagogical Features
- **Cognitive Processing Pause:** A forced 5-second transition between counting and tracing to allow the brain to "save" the quantity before shifting to the motor task of writing.
- **Anti-Overlap Logic:** A custom algorithm ensures dots never clump together, providing the "white space" emerging learners need to distinguish individual objects.
- **High-Contrast Design:** Accessibility-first colors and bold borders for ESE and ESOL support.
- **Privacy First:** Zero data collection. Offline-ready. Safe for any classroom or home environment.

## 🚀 The ThresholdEd Vision
We believe in stopping the "Factory Model" of sorting kids by age. By focusing on foundational mastery, we give students the "sweet spot" of learning—optimizing their Zone of Proximal Development (ZPD) to build lifelong confidence.
